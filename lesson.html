Lessons And Insight

Getting Comfortable With The Uncomfortable

At heart, I'm a builder and I love this. Sometimes these are little things like a new blog post or a small communication. other times, they come in the form of extensive programs or products to be shipped company-wide. There's always a bit of anxiety whenever I ship something, and depending on the risk involved with the project, that feeling grows. I can't change it, but all I can do is be prepared.

Curious

It's hard to imagine that it's been five years since I first started writing to you all. I've learned a lot in five years, and my life has also changed quite a bit since then. Thanks for the support, and I hope you have a chance to reflect on the past year — toast to a great 2020 and a new decade. Perhaps the number one reason I started this blog is out of curiosity. I was wondering, would anyone care to listen to what I have to say? Will anyone read it? Has anyone ever signed up for more content? I am curious. Wondering if I create a little corner on the internet where I can talk to other designers and share my journey with them, will anyone come up? Well, you did, and thank you!
